Finally, gas-powered vehicles generally require more maintenance and have higher operating costs compared to their electric vehicles. Gas vehicle engines have numerous moving parts that are unfortunately wearing out quickly, needs regular maintenance and repairs. Components like the engine, transmission, and exhaust system require periodic servicing, which can be costly and time-consuming. Additionally, gas-powered vehicles are less efficient in converting fuel into usable energy which I explained on the EV page and is called regenerative braking, resulting in lower fuel economy and higher fuel expenses over time. In contrast, electric vehicles have fewer moving parts and are more energy-efficient, leading to lower maintenance and operating costs.
